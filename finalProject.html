Final Project

CostaCalc

For my final project, I have created a calculator program which I am calling CostaCalc. We were instructed to create a program that either benefits the user through entertaining them, augmenting their learning experience or is helpful in day-to-day life. I decided on a calculator program because it fulfills two of those categories.

To use the program, it is as straight forward as any other digital calculator. You start out by pressing the buttons to type the number you want to input, although note that on this calculator it is limited to three decimal places. You can then choose the operation you want to perform, such as addition, subtraction, multiplication or division. With CostaCalc, you can continue to work with a number after you have pressed the equals button. If you press one of the four basic operations or the raise to a power button, it will use your current answer in your new problem. Otherwise, if you press a number button before you press an operation your past answer will be erased and the calculator will function as it normally would. In addition to these functions, CostaCalc also has the ability to solve sine, cosine and tangent functions. To perform those operations, you just need to press the respective button before you input your number. Finally, CostaCalc also has a clear button, which will clear anything you may have inputted so far.
